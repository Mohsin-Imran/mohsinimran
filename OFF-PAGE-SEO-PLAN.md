## 🔗 LINK QUALITY GUIDELINES

### What Makes a Good Link
✅ From high authority site (PA 30+)
✅ From relevant content (tech/developer focused)
✅ Anchor text contains keywords or natural text
✅ From reputable source with real traffic
✅ Diverse set of domains (not all from one domain)

### What to Avoid
❌ Paid links (Black Hat SEO)
❌ Links from unrelated content
❌ Spammy or low-quality sites
❌ Directory spam links
❌ Automated link building tools
❌ Private blog networks (PBN)
